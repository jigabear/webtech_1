Main characters of the Wheel of Time

The series contians five main characters, which are further split into two sections. There are three main characters which are the more important to the story, which are the original three from Emond's Field: Rand, Perrin and Mat. Egwene and Nynaeve are main characters, but the most important plot devices center around the three previously mentioned main characters. Rand, Perrin and Mat will be referred to as the three central main characters, whereas Egwene and Nynaeve are referred to as the supportive main characters.

Rand al'Thor

Rand al'Thor is one of the main characters of the series, and he is the most central one. He is the Dragon Reborn; the soul of the warrior of the Light, reborn into a new body. The warrior of the Light is supposed to be the one to fight the Dark One. The last known Dragon was Lews Therin Telamon, and his soul reincarnated into Rand al'Thor. Rand is known to have the strongest possible control over the male part of the Power, saidin. His power is categorized as ++1, which is the category given to only three characters in the series: Rand, Rahvin and Moridin. Rahvin and Moridin are both Forsaken, servants of the Dark One.

Perrin Aybara

Perrin is the second of the three central main characters. He is one of the Wolfbrothers; people that are able to telephatically communicate with wolves. One of the aspects of being a Wolfbrother is that they develop genetically to become similar to wolves as well. Due to his power as a Wolfbrother, he is greatly skilled in manipulating the world of dreams, Tel'aran'rhiod. Perrin is married to Faile ni Bashere t'Aybara, who is an apparent heir to Saldaea's vacant throne. Perrin was unofficialy known as the Lord of the Two Rivers, as he aided greatly in the defence of the region. Rand al'Thor later bestowed the title of Lord Steward of the Dragon in the Two Rivers to Perrin Aybara, making his title official.

Matrim Cauthon

Matrim Cauthon is the third of the three central main characters. He is rarely called Matrim in the series, and is almost always referred to as Mat. Mat is the Marshal-General of the Band of the Red Hand, which is an army named after legendary Manetheren heroes. Manetheren is one of the nations that arose after the Breaking of the World, and then destroyed subsequently during the Trolloc Wars. Mat is married to Fortuona Athaem Devi Paendrag, who is the empress of the Seanchan empire. The Seanchan have given him the name Knotai; the Prince of the Ravens.

Egwene al'Vere

Egwene al'Vere is the first of the supporting two main characters, one of the main female protagonists of the series. She is quite strong in her control of the One Power, which she has learned afer she has started training to become one of the Aes Sedai; an official faction for female practicioners of the One Power. Egwene is considered to be very similar to these Aes Sedai in nature during the novel; she becomes increasingly capable in manipulation, and assumes things that are not always true. She believes in her own knowledge, and that things must always be as they seem to her.

Nynaeve al'Meara

Nynaeve al'Meara is the second of the supporting two main characrters, one of the main female protagonists of the series. She is among the strongest users of the one Power, stronger than Egwene. She used to be a Wisdom in Emond's Field, where she was a talented village Healer, even though she felt obstructed in her usage of the Power early on in her life. Nynaeve is an Aes Sedai, part of the yellow Ajah, one of the different factions within the White Tower, the home of the Aes Sedai in Tar Valon.
