Glossary

Terminology Explanation
Aes Sedai Aes Sedai are those who wield the One Power. Since the Time of Madness, all of the Aes Sedai are women. Aes Sedai are the ones blamed for the Breaking of the World.
Age of Legends The Age of Legends was the age where the wielders of the One Power were the strongest. This age was ended by the War of the Shadow, which contained the event of the Breaking of the World. Aes Sedai were capable of extreme feats during this age.
Ajah The Ajah are societies among the Sedai, each following a different philosophy on the use of the One Power. These Ajah are denoted by different colors: Blue, Red, White, Green, Brown, Yellow and Gray. There is another hidden faction, called the Black Ajah, which serve the Dark One.
Breaking of the World The previous Dragon, Lews Therin Telamon, and his Hundred Companions resealed the Dark One's prison. As a result of this sealing, a counterstroke occurred, which tainted saidin, the male part of the One Power. Every male Aes Sedai went insane, triggering the Age of Madness. In their madness, these male Aes Sedai used their powers to greatly change the earth, destroying nearly everything around. This destruction of the world is referred to as the Breaking of the World.
Channeling Channeling is the controlling of the One Power.
Children of the Light The Children of the Light are a faction dedicated to defeating the Dark One and his Darkfriends. They were founded during the War of the Hundred Years. Through this war, they evolved into a fully military organization, and they hate the Aes Sedai because of their use of the One Power. Children of the Light are denoted by their uniform, consisting of white cloaks with a golden sunburst on them. This is why they are often referred to as Whitecloaks.
Dark One The name people use to refer to Shai'tan. As saying the real name of Shai'tan brings ill-fortune, Shai'tan is usually referred to as the Dark One. The Dark One was imprisoned during the Creation of the world, in a prison at Shayol Ghul. The attempted escapes from this prison have caused events such as the War of the Shadow, the taint of saidin, the Breaking of the World and the end of the Age of Legends.
Darkfriends People that follow the Dark One, in an attempt to obtain power and other rewards when he is freed.
The Dragon The name given to Lews Therin Telamon during the War of the Shadow.
Far Dareis Mai Translates to Maidens of the Spear. They are female warriors of the Aiel race. Maidens may not raise children themselves; having a child causes the child to be given to a different woman to raise, making sure the original mother is never known. The Maidens of the Spear may only focus on their spear. These children are important, however, due to a prophecy that a child born of a Maiden would reunite the clans of the Aiel, returning them to greatness.
The Five Powers The Five Powers refers to the different "threads", or elements within the One Power, that the users of the One Power use. These refer to the threads of Earth, Air, Fire, Water and Spirit. The more types of threads one is proficient with, the more powerful and versatile they are. Usually, users have a strong affinity with only one of the threads, sometimes two. Three threads are rare, and there have been none with all five threads since the Age of Legends.
The Forsaken Thirteen of the most powerful Aes Sedai ever, which have all gone to the Dark One's side during the War of the Shadow. They were promised immortality in return.
Gentling Gentling is the act of an Aes Sedai cutting of a male Channeler's access to the One Power. They can still sense the One Power, but they cannot make use of it. Regarded as necessary, as every male user of the Power ends up going insane after the tainting of saidin.
Myrddraal The Myrddraal are creatures of the Dark One that lead the Trollocs. They are offspring of Trollocs, but tainted by evil that made the Trollocs. This way they end up looking physically like regular men, but without eyes.
